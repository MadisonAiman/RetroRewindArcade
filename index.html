About Us

Welcome to Retro Rewind Arcade, your destination for all things retro! Step back in time with us as you play classic arcade games from the 80s and 90s.

From pinball to Pac-Man, our arcade boasts a carefully curated selection of games that will transport you back in time. Our facilities are also equipped with refreshments, seating areas, and special event hosting options, perfect for parties or corporate events. Don’t miss out on exclusive events like our high-score competitions and retro-themed parties!

Hours

Hours: 10 AM to 10 PM.

We host special events every Saturday night. Join us for tournaments, challenges, and themed nights!

Located in the heart of downtown Kansas City, Retro Rewind Arcade offers a blast from the past with a modern twist. Step back into the golden age of arcade gaming, enjoy classic titles, and immerse yourself in a retro gaming experience like no other.

Games

Explore our collection of retro classics, including Pac-Man, Galaga, Donkey Kong, and more! Our collection is constantly evolving, so you’ll always find something new and exciting to play.
